I made this character for SuperTuxKart, her name is Xochita. Everything, except for the concept art, was made in Blender. Luckily, she was accepted as and addon character and can be downloaded inside game. Project files are also available at online.supertuxkart.net

This is her concept art next to an unshaded Blender render of the character. Although the game uses PBR lighting, I still prefer the cartoon look.

The kart is obviously themed in butterflies. For the character, I simply wanted a pretty driver that could fit in the game.

Tris limit in SuperTuxKart is around 12000 for character+kart, and I basically hit that mark.

Textures are 512x512 pixels each (top row for character, bottom row for kart).

I rigged the character using Rigify addon, although added a few custom bones to control ears, tail and butterflies mainly.
